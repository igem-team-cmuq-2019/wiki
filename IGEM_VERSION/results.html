Results

Figure 1. Cas 12 produced by BL21 cells measured by western blot.

The BL21 cells were inoculated and grew to the mid-log phase (OD 30 Kletts) in LB+Amp+Chl medium when it was induced by IPTG for 18 hours. The proteins were harvested by being centrifuged by 5000 rpm for 5 minutes. The western blot shows Cas12 protein was successfully produced(circled in red).

Figure 2. Successful Cas12a Activity Observed

Cas12a was added to the reaction tube containing DNA template and a corresponding gRNA to analyze the indiscriminate cleavage activity of Cas12a. Fragments observed in lanes 2,6,8,10 display that Cas12a was successfully activated as the expected binding of template Cas12a occurred with the aid of gRNA.

While fragmentation was expected in lane 4 corresponding to the rs334_T mutation in the HBB gene, no fragments were observed reflecting the lack of the Cas12a activity possibly due to failure to form the Cas12a-gRNA complex or inability to identify the target mutated sequence.

Lane 11 - To test for off-target binding, we mixed Cystic fibrosis WT DNA template and the gRNA corresponding to the Cystic Fibrosis G mutation, and as expected there was no fragmentation observed which highlights the accuracy of the detection of the s75389940_G SNP
