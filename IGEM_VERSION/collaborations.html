Collaborations

We were mentored by iGEM Team Tubingen through how to model experimentally determined fluorescence values. They advised us to use machine learning by integrating a database of labeled DNA. As well as to consider DNA lengths for consistency and meaningful extrapolation of fluorescent value. We assisted iGEM Team Lund by integrating an ethical synthetic biology question into a campus-wide discussion and then reported the results back to them to gain insight on the perspectives of a diverse community. We also collaborated with Team Dresden to find a solution to wiki-related troubleshooting which they polished into a video and attended Team Marburg’s Golden Gate cloning Webinar. Finally we filled some surveys for insight such as that of Virginia iGEM Team where we provided information on the sustainability of resources in labs in Qatar and the usage of styrofoam boxes and their recycling

iGEM Tübingen

We were mentored by iGEM Team Tubingen through how to model experimentally determined fluorescence values. They advised us to use machine learning by integrating a database of labeled DNA, as well as to consider DNA lengths for consistency and meaningful extrapolation of fluorescent value, which inspired us to develop software of a mobile application to aid us in diagnostic purposes.

Virginia iGEM Team

We filled some surveys for insight such as that of Virginia iGEM Team where we provided information on the sustainability of resources in labs in Qatar and the usage of styrofoam boxes and their recycling.

iGEM Lund team

We assisted iGEM Team Lund by integrating an ethical synthetic biology question into a campus-wide discussion and then reported the results back to them to gain insight on the perspectives of a diverse community. The results were documented and shared with the group after the debate took place on the CMUQ campus. We also collaborated with Team Dresden to find a solution to wiki-related troubleshooting which they polished into a video and attended Team Marburg’s Golden Gate cloning Webinar.
