We were mentored by iGEM Team Tubingen through how to model experimentally determined fluorescence values. They advised us to use machine learning by integrating a database of labeled DNA, as well as to consider DNA lengths for consistency and meaningful extrapolation of fluorescent value, which inspired us to develop software of a mobile application to aid us in diagnostic purposes.
